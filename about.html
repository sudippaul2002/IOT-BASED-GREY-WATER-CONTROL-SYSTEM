Grey Water: What It Is, What to Avoid & More..

What is grey water? Grey water, also known as greywater or gray water, is recyclable wastewater produced from activities such as dishwashing, laundry, or bathing. Grey water differs from black water which is wastewater used in toilets and designated for sewage systems. Grey water can be used for a variety of purposes such as irrigation or toilet flushing and is collected from: Sinks, Showers, Bathtubs, Washing machines, Dishwashers,....
